Camille SCHWARZ is the Head of Personnel of the DiMiSch Company. Aged 19 and fan of TV series, she is a young woman committed to her work and her everyday life. After studying computer science and a master's degree in human resources, Camille worked in many companies before creating the DiMiSch Company with her two friends Diebold Aymerik and Quentin Millardet in 2017.
